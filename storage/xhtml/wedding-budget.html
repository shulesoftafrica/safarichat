Did you know that approximately half of your UK wedding budget will get spent on venue and catering? Did you now you can slash your costs if you hold your wedding on a weekday? Or an off-peak month?

Many couples also don’t realise that the number one most significant impact on their wedding budget is (wait for it) the number of guests invited. Fewer guests means lower costs, no matter which venue, photographer or florist you choose. So if you’re looking for areas to save, try reducing your headcount, and your catering costs will drop instantly.

Wedding Manager has put together a selection of budget-related tips and advice that teach you how to manage and get the most out of your wedding budget. We’ve got wedding hacks that translate into savings (i.e. a naked cake is cheaper than a frosted one!) With these tips in hand, you’ll be on your way to achieving the wedding of your dreams, within the budget of you can afford.
